Work With Us

At Arden Risk, we hold our people to the same standards we promise our clients: professionalism, discipline, and operational readiness at an elite level.

We recruit only the most capable, committed, and adaptable operators. If you believe you have what it takes, here's what we look for:

Core Requirements

  • PSIRA Registration (or eligibility to register)
  • Firearm Competency: Business firearm competency or Section 21 shoot approved
  • Medical Certification: FREC 3 (First Response Emergency Care) or equivalent
  • The specialized tactical/defensive driving qualification (private sector standard)
  • The legal PrDP needed in South Africa for goods/people transport
  • Clean criminal record & verifiable references

Preferred Backgrounds

  • Ex-Military or Ex-Police service
  • Graduates of Ronin SA or equivalent close protection programmes
  • Experience in executive protection, tactical response, or high-risk environments

Fitness Benchmarks

All operators are expected to maintain physical performance levels consistent with Australian Special Forces entry standards:

  • 2.4 km run in ≤ 12 minutes
  • 400 m swim fully clothed in ≤ 20 minutes
  • 50 uninterrupted push-ups
  • 60 uninterrupted sit-ups

These benchmarks ensure every operator can perform under pressure, in all environments.
